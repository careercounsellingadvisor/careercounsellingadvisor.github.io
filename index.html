Government Scheme

Bihar Student Credit Card

A visionary scheme by the Bihar Government to ensure that financial constraints don't stop you from pursuing higher education. Get an education loan up to ₹4 Lakhs with minimal interest rates.

Eligible Courses:

B.Tech / Engineering MBBS / Medical MBA / BBA BCA / MCA Nursing Polytechnic Law (LLB)

Documents Required

Student Copy
  • 10th & 12th Marksheet
  • Residential Certificate
  • Caste Certificate
  • Aadhar Card
  • Bank Passbook
  • 4 Photographs
Guardian Copy
  • Aadhar Card
  • Voter ID (Optional)
  • Bank Passbook
  • 2 Photographs
*Co-applicant must be parent/guardian.
