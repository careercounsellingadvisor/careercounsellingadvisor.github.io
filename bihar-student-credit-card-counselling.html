What is Bihar Student Credit Card Scheme?

The Bihar Student Credit Card (BSCC) is a visionary initiative by the Bihar Government. It ensures that financial constraints don't stop capable students from pursuing higher education.

Under this scheme, eligible students receive an education loan of up to ₹4 Lakhs with minimal interest rates to cover tuition fees, hostel charges, and books.

Who Can Apply?

BSCC Eligibility Criteria

Student Eligibility

Criteria for the applicant

  • Permanent resident of Bihar
  • Age limit: 18 to 25 Years
  • Passed 12th (Intermediate) or Equivalent
  • Should not have any other education loan

Course & College

Criteria for admission

  • Admission in NAAC/NBA/Govt recognized institute
  • Valid Admission Letter / Bonafide Certificate
  • Courses: B.Tech, MBA, Medical, Diploma, etc.
  • College must be approved in BSCC list
